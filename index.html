About Me

Where business, design & engineering collide

I'm Divyanshu, a builder from IIT Roorkee who studied Economics and ended up shipping real products that real people use. I got into PM because I couldn't stop asking "who is this actually for?" in every room I walked into. Eventually someone just handed me the roadmap.

Since then I've shipped across fintech, health-tech and research. Built RUPAIYA from zero for 100M+ underserved Indians. Automated 60+ hours of manual ops at a Bangalore fintech. Founded a SaaS startup with paying customers in week one. Led IIT Roorkee's Tinkering Lab for 3 years and mentored 1000+ builders. I move fast, think in first principles, and I don't ship things I wouldn't use myself.
